Carrot Cat Treats

Description

If your cat likes carrot for some reason, they'll probably like these treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon dried catnip
  • 3/4 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • Cold water as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In medium bowl add oil and mix in 1 cup of the flour until mixture looks sandy.
  3. Stir in catnip and carrots.
  4. Mix in egg. Add a little water if the mixture looks too dry and isn't holding together. Add a little flour if the mixture looks too wet and sticky. (Sometimes the carrots can be more wet or dry.)
  5. Sprinkle flat surface with a little additional flour and roll or pat out dough to Â¼ inch thickness. Sprinkle dough with additional flour as needed.
  6. Prick dough all over with a fork. Use pizza wheel to cut dough into 1/2 inch squares.
  7. Arrange squares on prepared baking sheet. Bake for about 12 minutes or until starting to brown.
