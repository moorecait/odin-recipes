Tuna Cat Treats

Description

Cats love tuna! Make sure to make them look cute, it's important to your cat(s).

Ingredients

  • 5 oz can tuna, drained
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 banana, mashed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degress F and tear off a piece of parchment paper.
  2. Mix all the ingredients. The dough will shape into a ball and be somewhat tacky.
  3. Place dough onto parchment paper.
  4. Cut another piece of parchment paper and place it over the dough ball.
  5. Flatten the dough out with your hand somewhat then roll out with a rolling pin until about 1/4 in thick.
  6. Place the parchment paper with flattened dough onto a cookie sheet.
  7. Remove the top piece of parchment paper and discard.
  8. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes until firm and golden.
